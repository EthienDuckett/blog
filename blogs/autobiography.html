Autodidact

Believe it or not, I spend most of my time at home programming or reading. It has affected me in two ways:
1. I learn phenomenally by myself.
2. I cannot tolerate mediocre materials and teachers.
I think that having become more picky has benefitted me a lot. If you would like to see how, then you can recommend that I learn something. I may or may not follow your advice, but of course you can motivate me with an opportunity such as a competition, collaboration, internship, and or job offer. I think I could stand to learn more about education, so I am compelled to beta test an online course and provide useful feedback.

School

I attend school at Caldwell Applied Sciences Academy high school and Caldwell Community College and Technical Institute. There are three academies at my high school, the one I am in is the Advanced Technologies Academy. In my academy, we learn about software. It is instructed mostly by Guy Meyers who you can find in the teacher's websites tab of the school website. The academies are part of a dual-enrollment program which is designed to prepare students for the future by giving them an opportunity to get college credits, some certifications, and an associates degree potentially before graduation.
